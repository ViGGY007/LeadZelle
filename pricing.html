Frequently Asked Questions

What does "done-for-you" actually mean? +
It means you don't have to learn a new software interface or drag-and-drop conversational flows. You tell us your qualification criteria, and we architect, write, build, and deploy the entire system on your site. We also integrate it directly with your existing tools.
How does the 48-hour setup work? +
Once you sign up, you'll complete a brief 10-minute onboarding form so we understand your services and questions. Within 48 hours, we will deliver the finalized bot flow logic and a script tag for you (or your developer) to drop into your website header. It's essentially a copy-paste process on your end.
Do I need a specific type of website? +
No. LeadZelle works on any website platform that allows you to add custom HTML/scripts—this includes WordPress, Webflow, Wix, Squarespace, Shopify, and custom-built sites.
What if I don't use Cal.com? +
Cal.com is highly recommended because it embeds native, interactive calendars inside the chat without taking users off the page. However, if you rely strictly on Calendly or Acuity, we can link out to those platforms instead.
Are there any limits on conversations? +
Both the Standard and Founding Member plans cover up to 2,000 chat conversations per month, which easily accommodates 95% of service businesses. If you go over, we will reach out to discuss custom volume pricing.
